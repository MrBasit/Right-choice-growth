Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When will I start seeing leads after joining RCG?

You start seeing leads from Day 1.

Q: Do you help with setup, or do I need to do it myself?

We handle the entire setup for you — from connecting your VA and Google Sheet to integrating AI follow-up systems. You don’t need to be tech-savvy. Once complete, you’ll start getting quality leads directly into your Google Sheet while building a database that grows over time.

Q: How are leads delivered to me?

Leads go straight into your Google Sheet, and you’ll also get instant notifications by text or email so you can follow up right away. Our automation continues working in the background during business hours to nurture your pipeline.

Q: Do we get scripts on how to follow up?

Yes. We provide you with proven scripts word-for-word so you know exactly what to say to book more appointments.

Q: What happens after I get a lead?

We deliver motivated, warm leads. Your role is simple: call, text, or leave a voicemail to book the appointment. Then show up ready to close.

Q: What if I don’t know how to get a listing or contract signed at the appointment?

If you’re new to the business, we recommend partnering with an experienced agent who can help you close these motivated leads. This allows you to build your name, brand, and confidence faster.

Q: What leads are we targeting?

• Expired & canceled listings
• Likely-to-move homeowners
• Probate & senior downsizers
• Distressed properties
• Absentee landlords
• For Sale By Owners (FSBOs)
